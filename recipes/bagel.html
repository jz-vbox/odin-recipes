Bagel Recipe

This is a bagel. This recipe will make a sandwich that is incredible and incredibly tasty. Great for your morning breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • Bagel
  • Deli meat
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Process

  • Cut your bagel and put it in the toaster
  • After it toasts, put on your sauces
  • Put two cheese slices on each half of the bagel
  • Start layering the bagel with the deli meat and vegetables
  • Put the two halves together (Press them hard if necessary to fit your jaw, or keep it open for an open faced sandwich)
  • Eat it
